CannaCalc — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

CannaCalc is an independent iOS app that calculates CANNA Coco feed recipes. This policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do with your information.

In one sentence: CannaCalc runs entirely on your device — it has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and makes no network connections at all; the only data it keeps is your calculator preferences, stored locally on your phone.

Who we are

CannaCalc is built and maintained by an individual developer ("we", "us"). The app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by CANNA. "CANNA" and all product names are trademarks of their respective owners, used only to identify the products the app calculates doses for.

What the app stores, and why

Calculator inputs (batch volume, source-water EC)Remember your last mix so you don't re-enter itStored locally on your device only

These are saved in the app's local settings (iOS UserDefaults) on your device. We collect this solely to provide the app's functionality. We do not use any of it for tracking, advertising, profiling, or analytics.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not operate any backend server, and the app makes no network requests.
  • We do not require an account, name, email, or any sign-in.
  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry about you or your usage.
  • We do not include advertising or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information — because none of it ever leaves your device.

Where your data is sent

Nowhere. CannaCalc works fully offline and does not transmit any data off your device.

Deleting your data

All of the app's data lives on your device. Deleting the app removes everything it has stored — there is nothing held anywhere else to delete.

Children

CannaCalc is a horticultural calculator and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
